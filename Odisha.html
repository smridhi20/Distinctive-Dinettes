Café Sundowner

Popular among residents of Bhubaneswar for their live gigs and upbeat vibes, Café Sundowner is probably among the most happening spots in the city. They feature a cool rooftop seating space where you can sit back with your gang while you gulp down a few drinks and tuck into some lip-smacking food. Their menu features an amalgamation of Thai, Chinese, Mexican and North Indian cuisines, so they have something for every taste bud.
