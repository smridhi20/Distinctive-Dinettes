Wasabi

A new arrival, Wasabi makes itself quite clear when it lists itself as a "fusion Pan Asian restaurant". Housed in Sector 7, Wasabi exudes a semi-formal vibe with its red themed interiors. But what is truly eye-catching are the murals that dot the restaurant's walls. There are many Instagram-able backdrops here.

Address: PQMW+PXR, Sector 7-C, Sector 7, Chandigarh, 160019

Phone: +919216669604

Cafe Nomad

Nomad specializes in Mediterranean food and many choices for vegetarians. We chose to share a mezze platter and tabouleh. Both are delicious. Service is excellent and prices reasonable.

Address:Sarovar Path, Sector 7-C, Sector 7, Chandigarh, 160019

Phone: 0172 654 1469
