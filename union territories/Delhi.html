Puppychino

Puppychino is a cute little dog-friendly cafe that is located in Shahpur Jat. Being Delhi's first dog cafe, there is a lot that you could expect from this place.For hoomans, they have pasta, pizza, cheesy garlic bread, shakes, and more. For doggos, they have Woofsome Cupcakes, Toothsome Pancakes with ice-cream, plain chicken breast, and of course, a lot more. Oh, and they can even arrange for a birthday party (at their puppychino farm) and can make a cake for your pet

Address:119, Shahpur Jat, Siri Fort, New Delhi, Delhi 110049

Phone: 096430 84323
