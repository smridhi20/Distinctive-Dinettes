OPA Kipos

OPA Kipos is a new fine dining restaurant that offers an immersive Greek experience that transcends beyond food.‘Opa!’ is an expression that a person says whenever a plate is dropped either by intent or by accident in Greece. Smashing plates during weddings or other celebratory occasions and screaming ‘Opa!’ is a custom unique to Greece.
