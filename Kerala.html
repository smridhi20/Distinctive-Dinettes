flotilla

The restaurant is built in Veli lake using wild jack wood, teakwood and coconut trunks. The structure spans more than 3500 square feet and can accommodate around 80 guests. The Flotilla provides the tourist a unique dining experience on the backwaters. They can enjoy the chirruping sounds of the birds and the calm atmosphere of the Lake. It is a two storeyed floating restaurant. The upper floor gives a better view of the lake and beach. The restaurant offers various snacks items including burgers, muffins, pastries, puffs, ice creams and drinks.The food is available at a reasonable price and the service is quite friendly.

Address: Lake, Veli, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala,695021

Phone: 085398 10606
