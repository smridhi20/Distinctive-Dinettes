Cafe Live & Loud

If you want to experience good music from local band, this is the place. A lively and young place to enjoy your drinks and snacks in evening. Place offers a mix of north-Indian and local snacks and food with reasonably priced drinks. Top it up with some peppy live band music.

Address: Tibet Road, Gangtok, Sikkim,737101

Phone: 096098 65570

Cafe Wanderlust

Café Wanderlust is for the quirky souls. ... Little pots of plants outline the café, a nice hammock swings right in the centre of the café. Moreover, they have also built a reputation of being called the cutest book café in Galleria Market.

Address: Hotel Dragon Inn,3rd Mile, near Tashi View Point, Gangtok, Sikkim 737102

Phone: 099297 62420
